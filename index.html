Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common queries about our trading, sourcing, and logistics services.

Need Assistance?

Our team is ready to assist you with sourcing, quotations, documentation, or logistics questions.

Contact Support

What products does SPARK INTERNATIONAL HK LIMITED trade?

We trade a wide range of consumer electronics, industrial equipment, textiles, household items, and general commodities. Our sourcing network covers verified factories across Asia and global distributors.

Do you offer customized or OEM products?

Yes. We support OEM and private-label projects. Clients can provide design files or specifications, and our team coordinates with suitable manufacturers to ensure proper quality and packaging standards.

Where is your company based?

We are based in Hong Kong SAR — a global trade and logistics hub that provides excellent connectivity for shipping and financial transactions worldwide.

Do you handle international shipping?

Yes. We coordinate international freight via air, sea, or courier. We can ship under EXW, FOB, CIF, DAP, or DDP terms depending on your requirements.

How can I place an order?

Send your inquiry or product list through our contact form or email. Our sales team will respond with a quotation, MOQ, and estimated lead time within 24–48 hours.

What are your lead times?

Lead times vary by product category and volume — typically 7–25 days for stocked goods and 30–45 days for customized production. We confirm timelines before order approval.

Do you provide tracking and shipment updates?

Yes. We share tracking numbers, shipping documents, and real-time logistics updates once your order leaves the warehouse.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer), L/C at sight, and selected digital payment options depending on the order size and buyer location. For first-time clients, advance payments are typically required.

Do you require deposits?

Yes, a standard 30–50 % deposit is required to initiate production or secure stock. The balance is payable before shipment or against the copy of the B/L or AWB.

Can I request a refund or replacement?

Refunds are handled case-by-case. If goods are damaged or do not match the agreed specification, we arrange replacement, re-shipment, or partial compensation depending on the situation.
